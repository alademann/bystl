Some friendly reminders…

  1. The studio will be open 30 minutes prior to and 30 minutes after each class.
  2. All classes are 90 minutes.
  3. Beginners are welcome and advanced students are challenged in all classes.
  4. First time students must arrive at least 15 minutes early for registration.
  5. Returning students please come 10 minutes early to sign in and allow your body to adjust to the heat.
