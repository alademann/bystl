Rebecca walked into a Bikram Yoga class 9 years ago with NO idea what she was getting herself into…

It was hot, difficult, and she loved it! She knew right away that this was something that she would, and did incorporate into her daily life. Rebecca’s passion has only grown over the years and transformed from being a dedicated student to (in 2006) becoming a dedicated teacher and student.

Rebecca can’t wait to see you in the hot room for a sweaty, moving meditation followed by an addictive post class state of bliss!
