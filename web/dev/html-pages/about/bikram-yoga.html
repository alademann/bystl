About Bikram Yoga

Bikram yoga is a demanding series of 26 postures (asanas) that works the entire body for 90 minutes.

The class is conducted in a heated room to improve circulation, help eliminate toxins, and significantly reduce the risk of injury during deep muscle stretching.

The series is designed for all levels, first time students and experienced practitioners alike.

Bikram Yoga: a union of the body, mind, and spirit.

Physically, Bikram Yoga is the perfect marriage of:

  1. Strength
  2. Flexibility
  3. Balance

Mentally, Bikram yoga trains the mind in five ways:

  1. To have faith in yourself
  2. To learn self-control
  3. To gain determination
  4. To improve concentration
  5. To build patience

In time, you'll learn to focus your mind and control your breath, allowing you to work harder, deeper, and calmer. With each class you will better learn the true meaning of Bikram yoga: a union of the body, mind, and spirit. To truly understand the magic of Bikram yoga you must experience it yourself first-hand.
